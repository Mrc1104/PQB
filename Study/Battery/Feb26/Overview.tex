
\begin{frame}{Overview}
	PVES experiment are sensitive to false asymmetries. It is imperative that we do not ``leak" helicity data out of the injector.

	\begin{itemize}
		\setlength\itemsep{1em}
		\item Goal:
			Can we pick up helicity information from the laser table, e.g. electronic crosstalk
		\item Method:
			Measure DC signals near the helicity source.
			\begin{itemize}
				\item Null asymmetry $\implies$  not leaking information
				\item Finite asymmetry$\implies$ leaking information
			\end{itemize}

				\includegraphics[width=100mm]{./Fig/Pick_Up_Test.png}
			\end{figure}
	\end{itemize}



\end{frame}
